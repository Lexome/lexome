A modern e-reader.
Lexome is an e-reader built for the future. Lexome's immersive features connect readers to authors' worlds and to the community of fellow readers.
Features
Summaries

Lexome's embedded summaries update dynamically throughout the book, helping busy readers keep track of the story without including spoilers from upcoming chapters.

Narration

Our text-to-voice narration service can turn any book into a read-along experience.

Live Chat

Lexome allows readers to create annotations on their device, share their notes, and participate in live discussions with other readers in their community.

Author AMA

Readers can add annotations to any part of the book with questions they have for the author. Any questions that the participating author answers will be viewable by the community.
